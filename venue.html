About Our Venue

Central Oklahomas Newest, Prestigious Wedding Venue

The Danforth at Sperry Lake Wedding and Event Venue showcases a delightful chapel, a captivating outdoor garden, and a spacious indoor reception area that is both enjoyable and accommodating. Whether you envision a breathtaking outdoor wedding ceremony, a whimsical nighttime reception illuminated by stars, or a significant milestone celebration, you have discovered the ideal venue! At The Danforth at Sperry Lake, you will find everything you desire in one place, nestled in the heart of Oklahoma. Your guests will be mesmerized by the enchanting atmosphere as they step into our magical garden, day or night. At The Danforth at Sperry Lake Wedding and Event Venue, you will always have a fantastic backup plan, regardless of the weather conditions. The best part is that you won't need to make a decision between an indoor or outdoor ceremony until your wedding day! Our ceremony spaces include a charming indoor chapel, beautifully lit outdoor gardens, a cascading waterfall fountain, and a grand floor-to-ceiling fireplace. Our expansive covered patio features charming bistro lights and a double-sided fireplace, setting the perfect mood for your special day. We are a comprehensive wedding venue that eliminates the stress of planning your dream day. We maintain an open vendor policy, making us Oklahoma City's most exquisite and inclusive wedding and reception venue. With the finest intimate indoor chapel and outdoor garden for your wedding, reception, or special event, all you need to bring is your dress... we'll take care of the rest!
